# Video Files for Scene 1

This directory should contain 6 placeholder video files for the surveillance camera feeds.

## Required Files

- `feed1.mp4` - Hallway surveillance footage
- `feed2.mp4` - Entrance surveillance footage
- `feed3.mp4` - Office surveillance footage
- `feed4.mp4` - Stairwell surveillance footage
- `feed5.mp4` - Parking surveillance footage
- `feed6.mp4` - Lobby surveillance footage

## Video Specifications

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds (looping)
- **Resolution**: 640x480 or 1280x720
- **Style**: Grayscale or low-light surveillance footage
- **Content**: Static scenes with subtle movement (for authenticity)

## How to Generate Placeholder Videos

### Option 1: Use the Generator (Recommended)

1. Open `generate-placeholder-videos.html` in your web browser
2. Click "Generate All Videos"
3. Download each generated video
4. Save them in this directory with the correct names

### Option 2: Use Your Own Videos

Place your own MP4 video files in this directory with the names listed above.

### Option 3: Use Free Stock Footage

Download free surveillance-style footage from:
- Pexels (https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/surveillance/)
- Pixabay (https://pixabay.com/videos/search/security%20camera/)
- Videvo (https://www.videvo.net/)

Convert to MP4 if needed and rename according to the list above.

## Notes

- Videos will loop automatically in the interface
- Lower quality videos (480p) are acceptable and will load faster
- The surveillance aesthetic works best with grayscale or low-saturation footage
